Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site through Google Analytics
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to deliver relevant advertisements based on your interests
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

Cookie Consent Notice

By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this Privacy Policy. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings or opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.
